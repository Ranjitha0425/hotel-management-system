Frequently Asked Questions

Do you have a dress code?

We recommend smart casual attire. While we don't strictly enforce a dress code, we ask that guests refrain from wearing athletic wear, beachwear, or overly casual attire.

Can I request a specific table?

We do our best to accommodate seating preferences. Please include your request in the "Special Requests" section of the reservation form, though we cannot guarantee specific tables.

Is there a time limit for dining?

We typically allow 2 hours for parties of 2-4 and 2.5 hours for larger parties. If you anticipate needing more time, please let us know in advance.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Yes, our chefs are happy to accommodate most dietary restrictions and allergies. Please note these in the "Special Requests" section when making your reservation.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require cancellations to be made at least 24 hours in advance. Late cancellations or no-shows may be subject to a fee of $25 per person.
