Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum spend requirement?

Minimum spend requirements vary by room and day of the week. The Wine Cellar starts at $1,500, while our larger spaces like the Manhattan Suite have a minimum of $5,000. Weekend bookings typically have higher minimums.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 4-6 weeks in advance for small rooms and 2-3 months for our larger spaces. Popular dates like holidays and weekends often book 6 months in advance.

Can I bring my own cake or decorations?

Yes, you may bring your own cake for a $25 cutting fee. Outside decorations are welcome as long as they don't damage the space. Please discuss your decorating plans with our events team in advance.
