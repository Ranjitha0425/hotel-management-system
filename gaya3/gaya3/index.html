Our Story

Founded in 2010, GAYATHRI HOTELS has become a culinary landmark in the heart of Manhattan. Our philosophy is simple: use the finest ingredients, prepare them with passion, and serve them in an elegant environment.

Our Executive Chef Michael Reynolds brings over 15 years of experience from top restaurants around the world, creating a unique fusion of traditional and contemporary cuisine.

Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or simply enjoying a night out, we strive to make every visit an extraordinary experience.
